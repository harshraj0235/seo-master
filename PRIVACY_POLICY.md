# Privacy Policy for SEO Master

**Effective Date:** May 31, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
SEO Master is designed to run locally in your browser. We prioritize your privacy and collect the minimum amount of data necessary.
- **Page Data**: When you analyze a webpage, the extension reads the public HTML elements (title, meta tags, headings, content, etc.) of the active tab.
- **Web Vitals**: The extension collects performance metrics (LCP, CLS, INP) directly from your browser.
- **No Personal Data**: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your name, email, IP address, browsing history (other than the active tab you analyze), or any other personally identifiable information (PII).

## 2. How We Use Information
- The extracted data is used strictly to generate SEO scores, technical audits, and AI/GEO readiness reports within the extension's side panel.
- All analysis logic (except for specific AI features) is executed locally on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services
To provide advanced AI-driven features (such as AI Snippet Optimization and SERP Gap Analysis), SEO Master sends anonymized text snippets to a free, public AI service (`text.pollinations.ai`). 
- No personal data or user credentials are included in these requests.
- These requests are only triggered when you actively click the respective analysis buttons in the extension.

## 4. Data Storage
- Analysis history and user preferences (such as Light/Dark mode) are saved locally on your device using Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- We do not have servers and do not store your data externally.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the Chrome Web Store listing.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the support tab in the Chrome Web Store.
